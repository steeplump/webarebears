About We Bare Bears

First airing on July 27th, 2015, We Bare Bears is a Cartoon Network original animated comedy focusing on the lives of three unique bear brothers. Created by Daniel Chong, the creator of the webcomic The Three Bare Bears, the story follows Grizzly, Panda and Ice Bear's attempts at humorously integrating themselves into the society of the San Francisco Bay Area.
